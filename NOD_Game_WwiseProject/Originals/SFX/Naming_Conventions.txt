Background Music (BGM): Lengthy tracks that may loop endlessly.
Format: BGM_Title_With_All_Words_Separated_By_Underscores_(VersionNo)
Examples:
- BGM_Main_Menu
- BGM_Tutorial_Boss
- BGM_Purple_Rain_v2


Sound Effects (SFX): Very short clips that confirm player's interactions with the game.
Format: SFX_Entity_Event_(State)_(VariationNo)_(VersionNo)
Examples:
- SFX_Violin_DamageTaken
- SFX_Violin_Footsteps_Water
- SFX_Violin_Footsteps_Sand
- SFX_Violin_Footsteps_Cement_01
- SFX_Violin_Footsteps_Cement_02
- SFX_Violin_Footsteps_Grass_03_v1
- SFX_Violin_Footsteps_Grass_03_v2


Music (MUS): Short tracks that highlight significant game events. Generally non-looping.
Format: MUS_(Entity)_Event_(VersionNo)
Examples
- MUS_LevelUp
- MUS_GameSaved
- MUS_Rhythm_SoundWave_v2
