Background Music (BGM): Lengthy tracks that generally loop endlessly.
Format: BGM_Title_With_All_Words_Separated_By_Underscores_(VersionNo)
Examples:
- BGM_Main_Menu
- BGM_Tutorial_Boss
- BGM_Purple_Rain_v2


Sound Effects (SFX): Audio feedback that highlight player's interactions with the game.
Format: SFX_Entity_Event_(State)_(VariationNo)_(VersionNo)
Examples:
- SFX_Violin_DamageTaken
- SFX_Violin_Footsteps_Water
- SFX_Violin_Footsteps_Sand
- SFX_Violin_Footsteps_Cement_01
- SFX_Violin_Footsteps_Cement_02
- SFX_Violin_Footsteps_Grass_03_v1
- SFX_Violin_Footsteps_Grass_03_v2


Jingles (JIN): Short tracks that punctuate significant game events.
Format: JIN_(Entity)_Event_(VersionNo)
Examples
- JIN_LevelUp
- JIN_GameSaved
- JIN_Skill_SoundWave_v2
